## **S.T.E.A.M. Lab**
![STEAM Lab](/images/science-lfa-updates/STEAM-Lab.png)

Click [here](https://go.gov.sg/nlb-events){:target="_blank"} for the latest programme listings. Stay tuned for more updates.
