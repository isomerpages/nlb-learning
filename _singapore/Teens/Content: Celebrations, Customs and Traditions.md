<h3><b>Learning Package</b></h3>
<h5 id="lp-making-sg" class="margin--bottom--lg"><b>Celebrations, customs and traditions</b></h5>
<p>Do you know how many festivals are celebrated in Singapore every year? How familiar are you with the myriad of religions, traditions and customs that are practiced here?  

Check out these books and resources to learn more about Singapore's diverse and colourful cultures.</p>
