<h3><b>Learning Package</b></h3>
<h5 id="lp- Celebrating%20Values" class="margin--bottom--lg"><b>Celebrating Values, Traditions and Culture</b></h5>
<p>Located at the crossroads of the East and West, Singapore has become home to many different ethnic communities today. What is exciting about being part of a multicultural society? The colourful festivals and delicious feasts of course! Come delve into some of the celebrations observed in Singapore. Discover interesting traditions and cultural practices that you never knew about!</p>
